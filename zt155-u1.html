Assist & Slipper Clutch


The race-inspired clutch uses two types of cams (an assist cam and a slipper cam), offering two functions. Along with lighter clutch lever pull, assist cam also enables the clutch torque capacity to be increased without gaining weight. When excessive engine braking occurs – as a result of quick downshifts (or an accidental downshift) – the slipper cam comes into play, forcing the clutch hub and operating plate apart. This relieves pressure on the clutch plates to reduce back-torque and help prevent the rear tyre from hopping and skidding.

All Terrain Tyres with Alloy Tubeless Rims

Complimenting the adventure motard styling are 110 mm (F) and 130mm (R) dual purpose tyres, which are the segment-widest adventure category tyres. These tyres not only perform great on tarmac but also in off-road and slippery conditions. Lightweight rims with side pull spokes ensures good impact resistance and enables use of tubeless tyres. Centre Mount Mono Shock

A centrally mounted mono shock softens the ride whilst delivering excellent control. Filled with high pressure nitrogen to ensure stability over long journeys and set with a small amount of pre-load, the rear suspension offers excellent comfort with reduced rider fatigue.

Automotive grade stainless steel exhaust

Coupled with ‘cool-touch’ aluminium anti-hot plate, the (underbelly/stubby) stainless steel exhaust adheres the mass centralization approach. The automotive grade unit is corrosion resistance and ensures long service life. The exquisite design of the exhaust system also allows beautiful sound beat that is music to the ears.

Ergonomic Seat

Utilising the latest memory foam technology, the seat cushion offers comfort, even for the heavier riders. Ergonomic shaping gives excellent support to reduce fatigue, even on the longest of rides. Split seats allows for pillion removal and luggage box fitment.
