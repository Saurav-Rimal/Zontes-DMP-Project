A high-tech motorcycle manufacturing company known for its precision engineering, winning the hearts of motorcycle enthusiasts across 100 plus nations.

An innovator in motorcycle design and manufacture within the Far East, Zontes Motorcycles strive to offer the best range of motorcycles possible in their class. Having been established in Europe since 2009, the current generation of models demonstrate an exceptional balance between quality and price, proving that you can buy a motorcycle which is value for money, not simply cheap. The current Zontes model range is diverse in style and spans 125cc to 350cc, with a firm focus on learner legal models. So whether you are looking for your very first geared bike, an everyday commuter or a lightweight recreational bike, Zontes have the bike for you.
